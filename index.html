Understanding the Landscape

About This Survey

The GCC Global Electrophysiology Capacity Survey represents a landmark effort to document and understand the current state of cardiac electrophysiology infrastructure, resources, and expertise across two critical regions.

By mapping capacity across Africa and Southeast Asia, we aim to identify gaps, facilitate knowledge exchange, and support the development of sustainable EP programs in resource-limited settings.
